Installation - Solity Platform

Follow these steps

If Solity Platform is already installed

Double Tap the home button and drag the Solity iOS Platform to the top of the screen. If you can't find the Solity iOS Platform, it may not be running.

1. How to Close Solity

Click on the blue install button below, to install the Platform.

2. Installing the platform

1. Select the 'Install' option 2. Press the 'home button' on your iPhone or iPad 3. Find Solity Platform to see it has completed installation.

3. Confirm Installation

A passcode will be required each time the platform runs. The first time you enter your passcode, you will need to confirm it a second time. Remember it (your 'administrator' can reset if you forget it or consult the user guides).

4. PassCode

You are now ready to login with your 'user' ID, you should have your 'user' name and password, enter it here, then press 'submit' button.

5. Sign In

Now you are ready to explore your organisation.

6. Welcome to Solity Software

Don't worry if you get the message 'Untrusted Enterprise Developer'. 1. Select 'Cancel'. 2. Go back and follow the step 3 instructions.

Follow these screen dumps for your new install

Click on the blue install button below, to install the Platform.

1. Installing the platform

1. Select the 'Install' option
2. Press the 'home button' on your iPhone or iPad
3. Find Solity Platform to see it has completed installation.

2. Confirm Installation

For iOS 9 Devices
1. Open the device "Settings" > General > Profile > and select "Solity Software Ltd".
2. Click the "Trust Solity Software Ltd" button on the top
3. Confirm by selecting "Trust" in the pop-up window.
4. Press Home button

3. Accepting Solity Software's profile

1. Open the Solity Platform from the home screen. 2. Select 'OK' to approve the sending & receiving of notifications on your iOS device.

4. Accepting Notifications

You are now ready to login to your 'organisation', you should have your 'organisation' name and password, enter it here. Note: You only enter your organisation name and password the very first time you login to solity platform. Press the 'activate' button.

5. Organisation Name & Login

A passcode will be required each time the platform runs. The first time you enter your passcode, you will need to confirm it a second time. Remember it (your 'administrator' can reset if you forget it or consult the user guides).

6. PassCode

You are now ready to login with your 'user' ID, you should have your 'user' name and password, enter it here, then press 'submit' button.

7. Sign In

Now you are ready to explore your organisation.

8. Welcome to Solity Software

Don't worry if you get the message 'Untrusted Enterprise Developer'. 1. Select 'Cancel'. 2. Go back and follow the step 3 instructions.
